# User Manual

## For Employees
**Objective**: Request and manage your assigned assets.
1. **Dashboard**: Upon login, view your assigned assets on the main dashboard.
2. **Maintenance**: If your laptop breaks, click **Report Issue** on the asset card. Fill out the description. You will be notified when a technician resolves it.
3. **Booking**: To book a conference room, navigate to **Bookings** in the sidebar. Select the room and the time slot.

## For Department Heads (Managers)
**Objective**: Oversee department assets and approve transfers.
1. **Transfers**: When an employee requests a transfer to another department, you will see it in the **Transfers** tab. Review the reason and click **Approve** or **Reject**.
2. **Audit**: During an audit cycle, you are responsible for verifying the physical presence of assets assigned to your department. Go to the **Audits** page and scan the QR codes of the assets on your floor.

## For Asset Managers (IT/Ops)
**Objective**: Manage the lifecycle of all organizational assets.
1. **Procurement**: Add new assets individually via **New Asset** or upload them in bulk.
2. **Allocations**: Assign assets to new hires.
3. **Maintenance**: Monitor the **Maintenance** queue. Assign technicians to open tickets and track repair costs.

## For System Administrators
**Objective**: Configure the system and monitor high-level health.
1. **Settings**: Manage organizational details, departments, and user roles in the **Settings** panel.
2. **Executive Dashboard**: Use the **Executive** tab to view AI-generated predictive maintenance alerts and overall asset utilization rates.
