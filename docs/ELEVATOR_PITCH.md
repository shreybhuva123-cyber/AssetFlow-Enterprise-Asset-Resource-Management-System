# Elevator Pitches

## 30-Second Pitch (The Hook)
"AssetFlow is a modern, AI-powered Enterprise Asset Management system. It replaces messy spreadsheets by automating the entire lifecycle of an asset—from procurement and employee allocation to maintenance and auditing. With built-in AI insights, QR scanning, and robust Role-Based Access Control, it’s the ERP SaaS that actually makes IT teams faster and saves companies money."

## 2-Minute Pitch (The Setup)
"Every mid-to-large enterprise leaks thousands of dollars a year in lost hardware, missed warranty renewals, and inefficient maintenance tracking. AssetFlow solves this. 
We've built a comprehensive SaaS platform on Next.js and Supabase that tracks an asset's entire lifecycle. Employees can request laptops, managers can approve transfers, and IT can run massive audits just by scanning QR codes with their phones. 
What makes us unique is our AI layer. We have a predictive maintenance engine that flags assets before they break, and a natural-language assistant that lets executives ask questions like 'How much are we spending on design software?' and get instant, data-backed answers. It’s highly secure, deeply integrated, and ready to deploy."
